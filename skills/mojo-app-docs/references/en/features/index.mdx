# Features

mojo brings together everything you need to manage your weight loss journey:

- **[Health Data Sync](./health-sync)** — Automatically sync data from Apple Health / Health Connect
- **[Body Measurements](./body-measurement)** — Weight, body fat, muscle mass, waist circumference, and visceral fat
- **[Medication Records](./medication)** — Injection logs, inventory management, and medication reminders
- **[Side Effects](./side-effects)** — 10 common side effects with severity tracking
- **[Import & Export](./data-backup)** — Backup & restore, CSV import, cross-platform compatible
- **[Goal Setting](./goals)** — Nutrition goals, hydration and sleep targets
- **[Daily Streak](./streak)** — Build healthy daily habits with streak tracking
- **[AI Feedback & Reports](./ai-reports)** — Real-time feedback and in-depth multi-expert analysis
- **[Inventory & Cost Analysis](./cost-analysis)** — Medication inventory tracking and cost calculation
- **[Account & Plans](../account/register)** — Registration, subscription plans, and referral rewards
