# About mojo

> Your AI health companion, helping you lose weight safely and effectively

mojo is a health management app built for people using **Mounjaro** or other **GLP-1 receptor agonists** (such as Mounjaro, Ozempic, Wegovy, etc.) for weight loss treatment. Available on **iOS** and **Android**, it integrates with Apple Health / Health Connect and uses AI to deliver real-time feedback and expert guidance.

---

## Why mojo?

When using GLP-1 medications for weight loss, you may have run into these frustrations:

- **Too long between appointments**: You only see your doctor once a month, and you have no idea what to do when questions come up day-to-day
- **Not sure if you're doing it right**: Your weight is dropping fast, but you don't know if that's good or bad
- **Data scattered everywhere**: Weight in one app, diet in another, exercise somewhere else
- **Unclear nutrition targets**: You don't know how much protein, carbs, or fat you should be eating
- **No expert guidance**: You want to adjust your diet or exercise, but aren't sure what's safe

**mojo was built to solve exactly these problems.**
