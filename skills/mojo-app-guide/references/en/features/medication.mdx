# Medication Records

mojo keeps a complete record of your GLP-1 medication journey, including injection logs, inventory management, and reminders.

## Injection Logs

Each time you take a dose, you can record:

- **Date and time**
- **Medication name** — supports Mounjaro, Ozempic, Wegovy, Zepbound, Saxenda, Rybelsus, and more
- **Dose** — standard dose options for each medication (e.g., Wegovy: 0.25mg, 0.5mg, 1.0mg, 1.7mg, 2.4mg)
- **Injection site** — thigh (upper-outer left/right), abdomen (left/center/right × upper/middle/lower), upper arm (left/right)
- **Linked inventory** — connect the log to an inventory item to track usage
- **Notes** — free-form text

For oral medications (such as Rybelsus), the injection site field is automatically hidden.

### How to Add a Log

1. Go to the Today tab → Dosing section
2. Tap to add a new injection log
3. Fill in the date, medication, dose, and other details
4. Tap Save

## Inventory Management

Track your medication supply to stay on top of remaining doses.

Each inventory item includes:
- **Medication name and type**
- **Total dose** (mg) or total count (pills)
- **Amount used** — calculated automatically from linked injection logs
- **Purchase price and currency** — supports TWD, USD, JPY, EUR, and 16+ other currencies
- **Date opened**
- **Progress bar** — visually shows remaining percentage

Depleted inventory can be archived without affecting your history.

## Medication Reminders

mojo automatically reminds you when it's time for your next dose based on your dosing frequency.

**How reminders work:**
- The next dose date is calculated from your last log date + your dosing interval
- If the due date arrives and no dose has been logged yet, a notification is sent
- If you've already logged a dose that day, no reminder is sent

**Example reminder messages:**
- Injectable (weekly): "Time for your Mounjaro! Last time was 2.5mg, injected in the abdomen (upper right)"
- Oral (daily): "Remember to take your Rybelsus! Last dose was 7mg"

**How to configure:**
Go to Settings → Notifications to set your preferred reminder time and toggle reminders on or off.

If you've stopped a medication (frequency set to 0), reminders for that medication stop automatically.
