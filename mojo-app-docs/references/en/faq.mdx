# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Basics

### Q: What is mojo?

mojo is a **weight loss management app** designed for people using GLP-1 medications (Mounjaro, Ozempic, Wegovy, etc.). It supports both **iOS** and **Android**. Core features include:

- **Injection tracking**: Log the date and dose of each injection
- **Progress charts**: Visualize changes in weight, muscle, body fat, and dosage
- **AI health analysis**: Real-time feedback and comprehensive health reports
- **Health data integration**: Automatically sync nutrition, exercise, and sleep data (via Apple Health on iOS, Health Connect on Android)
- **Water intake tracking**: Track daily hydration

### Q: Who made mojo?

mojo is independently developed by **HANA 花水木**.

### Q: Who is mojo for?

**Best suited for:**
- People using GLP-1 medications such as Mounjaro, Ozempic, or Wegovy
- Anyone who wants to track weight loss progress and body composition changes
- People who want personalized AI recommendations
- Those who value data privacy and comprehensive record-keeping

**Less suitable for:**
- People not using weight loss medications (the app can still be used, but some features won't apply)
- People who don't want to track health data

### Q: Does mojo cost money?

mojo is **free to download and use** for core logging features. Subscribing to mojo Friend unlocks advanced features:

- AI reports and real-time feedback
- Automatic health data sync (Apple Health / Health Connect)
- Data export and backup
- Medication inventory management
- Custom Streak goals

**Quarterly** and **annual** plans are available, with an early bird discount for new users.

## Health Data Sync

### Q: What data does mojo track?

| Data Type | iOS (Apple Health) | Android (Health Connect) |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Weight / Body fat | ✅ | ✅ |
| Steps / Active calories | ✅ | ✅ |
| Workouts | ✅ | ✅ |
| Nutrition (calories / protein / carbs / fat) | ✅ | ✅ |
| Sleep | ✅ | ✅ |
| Water intake | ✅ | ✅ |
| Menstrual cycle | ✅ | ✅ |

Injection records, side effects, and body measurements (muscle mass, waist circumference, etc.) are entered manually and do not rely on a health platform.

### Q: Is Apple Health / Health Connect required?

**No**, but it's highly recommended. mojo is designed for effortless logging: automatic sync of nutrition, exercise, sleep, and hydration eliminates manual data entry. It aggregates data from multiple apps and devices (MyFitnessPal, Apple Watch, Garmin, etc.).

Injection records, side effects, and body measurements support manual entry. Nutrition can also be entered manually or imported via deep link.

### Q: How do I authorize Apple Health? (iOS)

1. Open the mojo app
2. Go to the **Settings** tab → **Apple Health Sync**
3. Tap Authorize
4. Select the data types to share (enabling all is recommended)
5. Tap **Allow**

**To re-authorize** (if you accidentally denied access):
1. Open the **Health** app (iOS system app)
2. Tap your profile picture in the top-right corner
3. Find **mojo** and tap it
4. Enable read access for all data types

### Q: Which apps and devices do you recommend?

**Calorie tracking:**
- **MyFitnessPal** (free) — largest food database
- **Calory** (paid) — clean interface with AI food recognition

**Exercise & sleep:**
- **Apple Watch** — automatic exercise and sleep tracking
- **Garmin** — professional sports watch (syncs via Apple Health / Health Connect)
- **Oura Ring** — focused on sleep tracking

**Body composition:**
- **Omron HBF-702T** — body fat scale with Bluetooth sync
- **Inbody H30** — professional body composition analysis with Wi-Fi sync

### Q: Why can't I see muscle mass data?

Apple Health and Health Connect do not support "skeletal muscle mass" as a data type, so mojo cannot fetch it automatically. You need to enter muscle mass data manually in the app.

### Q: Will my Garmin watch data sync?

Garmin watch exercise and health data syncs first to **Apple Health** (iOS) or **Health Connect** (Android), and mojo then reads from those platforms.

**Note**: Some Garmin models may not write "Active Calories" to Health Connect. mojo handles this by falling back to "Total Calories Burned" for the calculation.

## Injection Tracking

### Q: How do I log an injection?

1. Open the **Today** tab
2. Find the injection progress block above the chart
3. Tap the block
4. Fill in the date, medication name, and dose
5. Tap **Save**

### Q: Can I edit or delete an injection record?

Yes. Go to the **Settings** tab → **Data Management** → **Injection Records**, then tap the record you want to edit or delete.

**Note**: Deleting an injection record will affect the progress charts.

## Body Measurements

### Q: How do I log weight, body fat, and muscle mass?

**Auto-sync (recommended):**
- Use a scale that supports Apple Health / Health Connect
- Step on the scale and the data syncs to mojo automatically

**Manual entry:**
1. Go to the **Settings** tab → **Data Management** → **Body Measurements**
2. Tap the **+** button in the top-right corner
3. Enter the measurement values
4. Tap **Save**

### Q: Why isn't measurement data showing on the progress chart?

**Possible reasons:**
1. **No injection records**: The chart uses injection dates as its reference points
2. **Measurement date outside the window**: Measurements must be within 3 days before or after an injection
3. **Measurement value is 0**: Check that the data was entered correctly

**Solutions:**
- Add at least 1 injection record
- Make sure measurements fall within 3 days of an injection
- Verify the measurement values are correct

## AI Analysis

### Q: What is Micro-feedback?

**Brief feedback:**
- Displayed at the top of the **Today** tab
- Auto-generated when data changes
- Real-time encouragement and reminders

**Example:**
> You hit your step goal today — great work! Don't forget to get enough protein.

### Q: What is the full AI report?

**Comprehensive analysis** (multi-paragraph):
- Displayed on the **mojo** tab
- Covers weight loss progress, nutrition, exercise, and sleep analysis
- Personalized recommendations and risk alerts

### Q: How often does the AI analysis update?

**Triggers:**
- Data changes (adding/editing nutrition, exercise, sleep, or measurements)
- 30-second cooldown period
- At least 5 minutes since the last analysis

### Q: Can I fully trust the AI recommendations?

**No:**
- AI recommendations are for **reference only**
- They cannot replace professional medical advice
- Consult a **doctor** or **dietitian** for any health concerns
- Medication dose adjustments must be made under **doctor's guidance**

## Progress Charts

### Q: What does the X-axis represent?

The X-axis is based on your injection record dates. Each injection date shows one data point, with the value coming from measurements taken within 3 days before or after that injection.

### Q: Why is there no data point for a particular injection date?

There are no measurements within 3 days before or after that injection. We recommend building a habit of measuring before each injection, or using a scale that syncs automatically.

### Q: How do I change the chart time range?

1. Go to the **Progress** tab
2. Tap the time selector above the chart
3. Choose: 1 month / 3 months / 6 months / All

### Q: How do I subscribe?

1. Go to the **Settings** tab → **Subscription & Account**
2. Tap **Upgrade Subscription**
3. Choose a plan (Quarterly or Annual)
4. Pay with Apple Pay (iOS) or Google Pay (Android)

### Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

**iOS:**
1. Open the **Settings** app → Apple ID → **Subscriptions**
2. Find **mojo** → **Cancel Subscription**

**Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store → Account → **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions**
2. Find **mojo** → **Cancel Subscription**

## Data Management

### Q: How do I back up my data?

Go to the **Settings** tab → **Data Management** → **Backup & Restore** to export all your data as a backup file.

The backup includes: injection records, body measurements, nutrition logs, water intake logs, side effect records, Streak settings, and more.

iOS and Android use the same backup format, so you can restore across platforms.

### Q: How do I delete all my data?

**Warning**: Deleting your account permanently deletes all cloud data!

1. **Back up first** (recommended)
2. Go to the **Settings** tab → **Subscription & Account**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm to proceed

### Q: How do I restore my data on a new device?

1. Install mojo on the new device
2. Sign in with the same account
3. Cloud data syncs automatically (injection records, subscription status)
4. Re-authorize Apple Health / Health Connect

## Account

### Q: How do I sign in to mojo?

mojo uses **Email + OTP verification**:

1. Enter your email address
2. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code
3. Enter the code to sign in

iOS and Android use the same sign-in method, and accounts are shared across platforms.

### Q: Can I be signed in on multiple devices at the same time?

Yes. Sign in with the same email on multiple devices — subscription status will sync across all of them.

## Other Questions

### Q: Can I share my progress?

Yes. Use the share feature on the **Progress** tab to generate a progress image. You can choose to **hide your actual weight** and only show how many kilograms you've lost and your trend graph, to protect your privacy.

### Q: How do I report an issue or submit feedback?

Go to **Settings → Support & Community** in the app. You'll find two options:

- **Report Issues or Feedback** — opens an email with your device model, OS version, and app version pre-filled
- **Feature Wishlist** — browse, vote on, or submit feature requests
