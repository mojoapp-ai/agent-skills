# Download the app

> mojo is free to download — get started right away

---

## System Requirements

### iOS

- **OS Version**: iOS 18.0 or later
- **Device**: iPhone (iPad is not currently supported)
- **Storage**: At least 100 MB of available space
- **Internet**: Required for initial setup and AI features

### Android

- **OS Version**: Android 9.0 (API 28) or later
- **Storage**: At least 100 MB of available space
- **Internet**: Required for initial setup and AI features

### Recommended

- **Apple Health / Health Connect**: For automatic health data sync
- **Smart scale**: A Bluetooth-enabled scale (e.g. Withings, Xiaomi Smart Scale)
- **Apple Watch or Garmin watch**: For more accurate exercise and sleep tracking

## How to Download

### iOS — App Store

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone
2. Search for **"mojo"**
3. Find the mojo app (developer: HANA 花水木)
4. Tap "Get" → confirm the download

**[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6751704399)**

### Android — Google Play

1. Open the **Google Play Store**
2. Search for **"mojo"**
3. Find the mojo app (developer: HANA 花水木)
4. Tap "Install"

**[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.hanamizuki.mojo)**

---

## After Installation

### First Launch

1. Tap the mojo icon to open the app
2. Read and agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
3. Choose whether to allow push notifications (recommended — you'll receive real-time AI feedback)
4. Complete the initial setup

### Initial Setup Includes

- **Basic info**: Gender, date of birth, height
- **Weight goal**: Starting weight, target weight, target date
- **Activity level**: Sedentary, lightly active, moderately active, highly active
- **GLP-1 usage**: Whether you're currently using it, medication type, dosage

Once setup is complete, you're ready to start using mojo!

## FAQ

### Why can't I find mojo in the store?

Possible reasons:
1. **Regional availability**: mojo may only be available in certain regions
2. **Device incompatibility**: Make sure your OS meets the minimum requirements (iOS 18.0 / Android 9.0)
3. **Search terms**: Try searching for "mojo" directly

### Is mojo free to download?

Yes. mojo is **free to download** and includes basic features at no cost.

Some advanced features require a mojo Friend subscription, including:
- AI analysis and real-time feedback
- Automatic health data sync
- Data export and backup

### Can I use mojo offline after downloading?

Some features work offline:
- ✅ **Offline**: Logging weight, food, exercise, and injections
- ❌ **Requires internet**: AI analysis, health data sync

## Updates & Maintenance

### How do I update mojo?

We recommend enabling automatic updates to ensure you're always on the latest version.

### How do I check my current version?

Open mojo → "Settings" tab → scroll to the very bottom to see the version number.

### What should I do if I run into a problem?

1. **Check your connection**: Make sure Wi-Fi or mobile data is working
2. **Restart your device**
3. **Report the issue**: Go to Settings → Support & Community → Report an Issue or Suggestion
