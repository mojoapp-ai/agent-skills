# Side Effects

mojo lets you log side effects during your medication journey, helping you track symptom patterns and giving your doctor useful reference data at follow-up appointments.

## Supported Side Effect Types

**Gastrointestinal (7 types):**
- Nausea, Diarrhea, Loss of appetite, Vomiting, Constipation, Indigestion, Abdominal pain

**Other (3 types):**
- Fatigue, Dizziness, Headache

## Severity Levels

Each side effect can be marked with one of three severity levels:
- **Mild** — tolerable, does not affect daily activities
- **Moderate** — noticeable discomfort
- **Severe** — significantly impacts daily life

## How to Log

1. Open the Side Effects page
2. Select a date (you can switch to a past date to fill in missed entries)
3. Tap the severity button for each side effect (tap again to deselect)
4. Use the notes field to record any other symptoms or observations
5. Saves automatically — no need to tap a save button

Only one record exists per day. Editing replaces the previous entry.

## How Your Data Is Used

- Side effect logs from the past 13 days are included in AI analysis to make recommendations more accurate
- View all records by month in Data Management
- Side effect records are included in backups and support cross-platform restore
