# Daily Streak Goals

Streak tracks whether you hit your health goals each day and encourages you to keep your streak alive. Like daily quests in a game, completing them builds up your consecutive days count, helping you turn healthy habits into a daily routine.

## Trackable Items

You can freely choose which items to track:

- **Calorie goal** — Whether daily calorie intake is within the target range (configurable tolerance: ±10% / ±15% / ±20%)
- **Protein goal** — Whether daily protein intake meets the target
- **Exercise** — Choose from: any exercise / strength training / cardio
- **Sleep hours** — Default 7 hours, customizable
- **Steps** — Default 5,000 steps, customizable

## Completion Modes

Choose a completion mode that matches your goal intensity:

| Mode | Description | Best for |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Any one** | Meeting any single item counts as a success | Just starting to build habits |
| **At least N** | Customize how many items must be met | Gradually raising the bar |
| **All items** | Every selected item must be achieved | High standards for yourself |

> It's recommended to start with "Any one" and increase the difficulty once your habits are stable.

## Streak Statistics

- **Current streak** — How many consecutive days you've achieved your goal
- **Longest streak** — Your all-time best record
- **This week** — Your performance this week
- **This month** — Your performance this month

## Streak Freeze

Each month you get a limited number of "get out of jail free" cards. If you miss your goal on a particular day, you can use a Freeze to prevent your streak from resetting.

This is designed to avoid losing motivation from a single missed day — taking an occasional rest day is a healthy part of any routine.
