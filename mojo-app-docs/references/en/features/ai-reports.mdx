# AI Feedback & Reports

mojo offers two types of AI analysis: lightweight real-time feedback and comprehensive in-depth reports.

## Real-Time Feedback

After each health data update, mojo delivers a short, personalized feedback message at the top of the home screen within seconds.

**Trigger conditions:**
- Logging or updating any data — nutrition, exercise, sleep, body measurements, injection records, side effects, menstrual cycle, etc.
- The system detects a meaningful change in the data (not just re-opening the same record)
- At least 15 minutes have passed since the last feedback

**Example feedback:**
> You hit your step goal today — great work! Don't forget to get enough protein.

Tap to expand and read the full feedback.

## Full Report

Go to the "mojo" tab and tap "Start AI Analysis" to get a comprehensive health analysis report.

The AI synthesizes your health data from multiple angles — including medication safety, nutrition intake, exercise performance, and overall health — to provide specific, actionable recommendations.

### Report Structure

1. **Achievements** — What you've been doing well
2. **Immediate actions** — Things to adjust right away
3. **Short-term goals** — Recommendations for the next 1–2 weeks
4. **Long-term plan** — Direction for the next 1–3 months

### Data Range for AI Analysis

The analysis draws from your recent health data:
- Last 10 body measurements
- Last 3 days of nutrition logs
- Last 7 days of exercise records
- Last 14 days of sleep and side effect records
- Complete injection history
- Last 6 menstrual cycles (female users)

The more complete your records, the more accurate the AI's recommendations will be.

### Tips

The side effect log page has a notes field. In addition to checking off symptoms, you can use it to write how you're feeling physically or any thoughts for the day. This text is also included in the AI analysis, making the recommendations more relevant to your actual situation.

For example: "Less appetite today but feeling energetic", "Started strength training this week", "Follow-up appointment next week to ask about dosage adjustment".

## Important Disclaimer

AI recommendations are for **reference only** and cannot replace professional medical advice. Consult your doctor for any health concerns, and any medication dosage adjustments must be made under a doctor's guidance.
