# Sign Up & Login

## How to Sign In

mojo uses Email + OTP verification — no password required:

1. Enter your email address
2. You'll receive a 6-digit verification code
3. Enter the code to sign in

Your first sign-in automatically creates your account. No separate registration step needed.

## Using Multiple Devices

iOS and Android share the same account. You can be signed in on multiple devices simultaneously. Subscription status syncs automatically.

## Deleting Your Account

Go to Settings → Subscription & Account → Delete Account.

Once deleted, all cloud data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered. We recommend [exporting a backup](../features/data-backup) first.
