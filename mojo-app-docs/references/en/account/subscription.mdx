# Upgrade to mojo Friend

mojo's core features are free to use. Upgrading to **mojo Friend** unlocks the full suite of AI analysis and advanced features.

## Plans

| Plan | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Quarterly (3 months)** | Auto-renews every three months |
| **Annual (1 year)** | Best value |
| **Early Bird (1 year)** | New users only — valid within 21 days of first opening the subscription page |

All plans renew automatically and can be cancelled at any time.

## How to Upgrade

1. Go to Settings → Subscription & Account → Upgrade Subscription
2. Choose a plan (Quarterly, Annual, or Early Bird)
3. Pay with Apple Pay (iOS) or Google Pay (Android)
4. Access unlocked immediately

### Upgrading via Official Link

We sometimes share the official link [mojoapp.ai/subscribe](https://mojoapp.ai/subscribe) (for example, in our newsletter).

- If you already have mojo installed, tapping the link opens the subscription page directly in the app.
- If you don't have the app yet, the link brings you to this page so you can learn about the plans first.

## How to Cancel

**iOS**: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → find mojo → Cancel Subscription

**Android**: Go to Google Play → Account → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → find mojo → Cancel Subscription

After cancellation, you keep access until the end of the paid period, then revert to the free plan.

## Gift Subscriptions

You can purchase a gift subscription for a friend:

1. Go to Settings → Subscription & Account → Buy Gift Subscription
2. After payment, you'll receive an 8-digit redemption code
3. Share the code with your friend

Your friend goes to Settings → Subscription & Account → Redeem Gift, and enters the code to activate. If they already have an active subscription, the time will be added to their existing plan.
